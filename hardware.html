Introduction
After understanding the seriousness of vitamin D deficiency, we decided to implement our solutions into the easy-to-use fermentation kit and the recipe to solve the issue.
Description
The fermentation kit includes a pounding tool, a waterless airlock top, a fermentation jar, and most importantly, our fermentation starter. With the help of fusion 360, a 3D-modeling software, we were able to design the complete model of the numerous parts of the kit. After adding the food ingredients with the fermentation starter into the fermentation jar, the food would process through proper fermentation. With this fermentation starter, the ability of probiotics to produce butyrate would be enhanced.
Our kit also includes recipe books that will aid customers in their fermentation process, allowing them to make d-licious fermented foods with ease. Recipe books contain ingredients as well as clear instructions that cover in detail how to make different fermented foods. Benefits & Advantages
All the equipment within the fermentation kit is reusable, and the materials are sturdy and safe. The fermentation kit is also easy to use. The team combined all the advantages of a fermentation kit and the rising popularity of e-commerce, hoping to solve the problem of vitamin D deficiency and provide a better solution to vitamin D deficiency for people all around the world.
