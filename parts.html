Description
Our assigned part numbers are BBa_K3729000 to BBa_K372999. Our parts collection consists of Beta-hydroxybutyryl-CoA dehydrogenase sequence and different Butyryl-CoA dehydrogenase sequences as well as their expression constructs. Different promoters and RBSs are inserted in front of the ORFs and terminators at the end of the ORFs to form expression constructs upregulating the expression of each enzyme. This parts collection provides various methods to maximize the synthesis of butyrate and ultimately the binding activity of vitamin D receptors.
